The Best Movies according to Pedro

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Lord of the Rings

Epic high fantasy action combined with intricate storytelling, an engrossing plotline, deep character development across their entire roster, and an unforgettable musical score.

Fierce Creatures

A sexy and funny adventurous romp detailing the wild lifestyles of the employees at Octopus Zoo as they strive to keep the zoo open amidst megaconglomerate corporate billionaire Rod McCain. A crowd-pleasing display of fierce creatures to behold, and no we don't mean the animals. From the excellent ensemble cast that brought you "A Fish Called Wanda".

The Passion of the Christ

A brutally and hauntingly accurate depiction of the final hours of Jesus's life on Earth. Starring Jim Caviezel and directed by Mel Gibson.
